### 1. 🤝 AI-Powered Recruitment & HR Management Tool

> *+ Skill Assessment & Hiring Test Platform*

**The Problem:** HR teams waste 60-70% of time on manual resume screening, scheduling, and assessment. Bias in hiring, poor candidate experience, and fragmented tools kill productivity.

**Market Gap:** No unified AI-native HR suite that combines recruitment, skill testing, onboarding, and analytics in one platform for SMBs and enterprises in Tier 1/2 markets.

**USP:**
- AI resume screening with bias detection
- Auto-generated skill assessments per job role
- Video interview analysis (tone, confidence, answer quality)
- Smart candidate ranking & predictive hire scoring
- ATS + HRMS in one platform

**Customer Pain Points:**
- Too many tools (ATS + assessment + interview scheduler = 3 separate platforms)
- High cost of enterprise HR tools (BambooHR, Workday too expensive for SMBs)
- Lack of skill-based hiring intelligence

**Revenue Streams:**
- SaaS subscription (per seat / per hiring volume)
- Pay-per-assessment credits
- White-label licensing for staffing agencies
- API access for HRMS integrations

**Future Features:** AI culture-fit scoring, psychometric testing, diversity hiring dashboard, predictive attrition alerts

**Investment Required:** ~$400K–$600K (MVP to full launch)
**Timeline:** 90–120 days for MVP | 180 days for full v1.0
